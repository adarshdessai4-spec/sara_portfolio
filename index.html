About Sara

Sara is a high-fashion and commercial model celebrated for her refined presence on runway and in front of the camera. She brings a balanced blend of elegance and strength, adapting seamlessly to the visual language of each campaign.

From luxury editorials to contemporary lifestyle stories, Sara collaborates closely with creative teams to deliver narratives that feel authentic and captivating. Her experience spans collaborations with designers, beauty houses, and global lifestyle brands.
